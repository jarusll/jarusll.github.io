A bit about Consistency 🔗

I have decided I wanna get good at Digital Art. Art is hard, it’s tough work. I want to integrate it in my life and make it second nature. I want to miss it when I am not drawing.

There’s no secret recipe to it, but brutal consistency. And so, I have decided to draw a lot of bosoms. This piece is a promise to myself.

I will start with line works, followed by depth. Maybe painting then, and then composition. Lets make it happen.
